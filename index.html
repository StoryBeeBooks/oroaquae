Ting Ting Gao

The Beautiful Journey

Toronto

Ting Ting Gao lives and works in Toronto, Ontario. Over a long artistic career, the artist has developed a psychologically charged painting practice that addresses subjects often overlooked by society while pursuing a broader ambition: to capture the essence of the human spirit.

Contemporary Artist

Shelley Adler

Fluid Expressions

Vancouver

Shelley Adler is a contemporary artist based in Vancouver, British Columbia. Her work explores the intersection of color, form, and emotion through dynamic abstract compositions. Using bold palettes and fluid techniques, Adler creates visual narratives that invite viewers into contemplative spaces where intuition and artistic expression converge.
