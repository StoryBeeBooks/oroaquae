About Oro & Aquae

Oro & Aquae is a contemporary art gallery dedicated to showcasing exceptional artists and their works. We believe in the power of contemporary art to inspire, challenge, and transform perspectives. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between artists and collectors through carefully curated exhibitions and engaging programming.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant community of artists, collectors, and art enthusiasts united by a passion for contemporary art. Through our exhibitions, we aim to promote emerging and established artists while fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for contemporary artistic practices.

Our Gallery

Located in Vancouver, Oro & Aquae features contemporary artworks across various mediums and styles. Our gallery space is designed to provide an intimate setting where visitors can engage directly with the art and artists.
